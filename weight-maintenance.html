About the Program

The Weight Maintenance Plan is designed for individuals who have already achieved their weight goals and want to maintain their progress sustainably without rebound weight gain.

Who Should Choose This Plan?

  • People who have recently lost weight
  • Individuals looking to stabilize their weight
  • Those who want long-term lifestyle balance

What You Get

  • Balanced maintenance diet plan
  • Portion control guidance
  • Lifestyle and habit support
  • Periodic progress reviews

Program Duration

4 weeks / 8 weeks (customizable)
