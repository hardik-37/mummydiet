Weight Loss Program

Obesity is caused by eating an imbalanced diet which is not only high in calories but lacks essential nutrients. Many times, ignorance or emotional eating and stressful life leads to overeating, thus resulting into overweight.

If obesity is not controlled well on time, it can lead to many health problems like diabetes, heart disease, high blood pressure, gallbladder disease, fatty liver and cancer as well.

If you have no medical conditions and hormonal imbalances, in this weight reduction program we will help you to reduce your weight in a progressive way with a balanced diet. You will develop mindful eating habits with many options as per your personal likings and choices.

You will find an increase in body stamina, strength and immunity and this will lead to better life forever.

This program can help to reduce 3–5 kg per month initially and later it can be 2–4 kg depending on how sincerely you follow and adopt the changes given. No target loss is assured; it all depends on your will and determination.

Program Features and Sequence

  • Dietary recall, BMI calculation and blood parameters checking (if required)
  • Deep dive consultation and customized diet plan as per individual need
  • Guidance on correct portion sizes and selection of foods
  • Key focus on overcoming nutrient deficiencies
  • Follow-up call every 10 days and WhatsApp support
  • Focus to avoid artificial or synthetic food items
  • More focus on homemade or easily available options
  • Monthly nutrition tips to improve better food life balance
  • Prepare for a long-term food selection and develop good dietary habits
  • Maintenance diet plan for life
